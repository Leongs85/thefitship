The exercise and the excursion are of equal importance

The Training Deck

Elite S&C, open to the sky

80–100 metres of converted open deck. 20+ fully kitted power cages, marked sprint tracks, sled lanes, and full conditioning stations. Your programme, your layout. Mediterranean light overhead and nothing but coastline on the horizon.

The Accommodation

Recovery built into the design

Restorative quarters crafted for the athlete who takes sleep as seriously as the session. Compact, immaculate, and considered. A private space that works as hard as you do.

The Fuel Station

Performance nutrition, executed

Performance chefs who build menus around your athletes' individual nutrition protocols. Diet, timing, macros: pre-session, post-session, and every window in between.

The Social Deck

Where the squad comes together

The upper deck is the social heart of the ship. Yoga at sunrise. Stretch sessions with the coast on the horizon. Team debrief as the sun goes down. The kind of environment that bonds a squad for the season ahead.

Off-Board Adventures

Every port, on your terms

When we moor, the destination is yours. Take a structured session: beach sprints, coastal trail runs, open-water work, or a guided climb. Or simply go. Explore the old town, find a market, swim, eat, wander. The itinerary is a suggestion. The experience is entirely your own.
